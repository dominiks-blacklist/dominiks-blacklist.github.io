About the Show

"The Blacklist" is a thrilling crime drama that follows the enigmatic Raymond "Red" Reddington, a former U.S. Navy officer turned high-profile criminal. After decades of eluding capture, Red mysteriously surrenders to the FBI with an intriguing offer: he will help catch the world's most dangerous criminals, under one condition - he must work with rookie FBI profiler Elizabeth Keen. As they dive deeper into the criminal underworld, the line between ally and enemy blurs, and shocking secrets are unveiled.

Episode Guide

Episode 1: Pilot

Ex-government agent and one of the FBI's Most Wanted fugitives, Raymond "Red" Reddington mysteriously surrenders to the FBI with an offer to help catch dangerous criminals. His condition: he must work directly with new profiler Elizabeth Keen.

Episode 2: The Freelancer

Red and Liz work together to stop a dangerous assassin known as "The Freelancer" from carrying out his next hit. As they delve deeper into the case, Liz begins to question Red's true motives for choosing her as his partner.

Episode 3: Wujing

Red brings a new Blacklister case to the FBI, sending Liz and Ressler on an undercover mission to take down a dangerous Chinese spy. Meanwhile, Liz struggles to trust Red as she tries to keep her marriage to Tom from falling apart.
