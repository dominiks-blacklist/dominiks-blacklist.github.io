The Blacklist: Unraveling the Mystery

"The Blacklist" follows Raymond "Red" Reddington, a former U.S. Navy officer turned high-profile criminal, who voluntarily surrenders to the FBI after eluding capture for decades. His only condition: he must work with a newly minted FBI profiler, Elizabeth Keen, to bring down dangerous criminals and terrorists.

As the series unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the line between good and evil becomes increasingly blurred. Welcome to a world where trust is a luxury and everyone has something to hide.
